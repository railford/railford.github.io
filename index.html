About Fox Fleet Services

Fox Fleet Services serves Northridge and its surrounding communities. Our fleet service program handles every aspect of vehicle maintenance with strong emphasis placed on preventative measures. Our knowledge, combined with state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, will ensure that your vehicles (regardless of make/model) are accurately maintained.

What does Fox Fleet Services offer?

  • Vehicle maintenance
  • 24 months/24,000 warranty
  • Priority/same day service
  • Flexible credit/payment plans
  • Vehicle customization
  • Towing services
  • Complimentary pickup and delivery
  • Affordable pricing

Q&A: Randy Mark (Owner)

Q: Why should a commercial business entrust you with their Fleet?
A: Because we do the job right the first time!

Q: What got you interested in the automotive field?
A: I've always loved automobiles and motorcycles. My first automotive class was in high school in 1979.

Q: How long have you been a professional mechanic?
A: 34 years.

Q: Where did you train professionally?
A: West Valley Occupational Center. I graduated with a certificate in automotive technologies in 1983. I then went back to school in the '90s and received my ASE Master Technician certification. I also have an L1 Advanced engineer performance certificate licensed by the state of California to do smog emission diagnostics and repairs.

Q: How can a business save money on mechanical repairs?
A: Performing scheduled maintenance on time is the best way to prevent costly repairs. And regardless of what service we perform, our technicians must complete a thorough multi-point check inspection before I will allow a vehicle to leave our premises.
